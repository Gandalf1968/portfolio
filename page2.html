A little about myself

I grew up in North Glengarry County in Ontario, Canada. I attended Laggan Public school, from K-8, then 9-12 at Glengarry District High School in Alexandria, Ontario.

After high school I took a year off and worked out in BC. I then came back and enrolled at St. Lawrence College in Cornwall, Ontario, where I would meet my future wife. When she graduated from nursing and took a job in southern Texas, I went with her. We spent 5 years there, have 2 kids.

We then moved to Macklin, Saskatchewan where we lived for 23 years.
